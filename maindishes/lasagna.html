Lasagna

    Ingredients
  • 2 - Lasagna Pasta
  • 5 - 29 oz cans of tomato sauce
  • 1 or 2 - 6 oz cans of tomato paste
  • 2 - 32 oz bags of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 - 24 oz small curd cottage cheese
  • 3 pounds hamburger meat
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Oregano
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil
    Directions
  1. Brown the hamburger meat and drain the grease
  2. Combine hamburger meat and tomato sauce. Add tomato paste depending on how watery it is.
  3. Add in Pepper, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, and Oregano
  4. Cook on medium heat, being mindful to stir. Make sure to put a lid on and leave it open a little
  5. Once it cooks, add in more seasoning based on taste/smell.
  6. You can boil pasta while sauce is cooking or wait until after. Mix water and some vegetable oil and bring to a boil before putting the pasta in. The vegetable oil will keep the pasta from sticking together.
  7. Once sauce and noodles are ready, get a large, deep pan. you will start with a light layer of sauce on the bottom so it won't stick.
  8. After that, you will put a layer of noodles on top and cover that layer with sauce
  9. You will then add in cottage cheese on top and then mozzarella cheese
  10. You will then continue to layer. This recipe and size pan allows for 4 layers.
  11. Make sure to preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cover the pan with foil so the cheese doesn't burn
  12. Bake in oven for 40 minutes to an hour with the foil, then another 20 minutes or so without the foil to let the cheese brown.
